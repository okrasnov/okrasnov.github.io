Polarization of EM Waves Fully Polarized Wave in different Polarization Basis  

This module presents the representation of the arbitrary fully polarized electromagnetic wave in different polarization basis:

E = E1 e1(theta,tau) + E2 e2(theta+pi/2,-tau)

where E is the wave with arbitrary polarization, E1 - coplex amplitude of the ortihogonal polarization basis component's e1(theta,tau), E2 - coplex amplitude of the ortihogonal polarization basis orthogonal component's e2(theta+pi/2,-tau). Here 'theta' is the angle of orientation of one elliptical component of polarization basis, which has the ellipticity angle 'tau'.
