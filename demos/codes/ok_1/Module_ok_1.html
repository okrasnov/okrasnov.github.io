Polarization of EM Waves Polarization Ellipse of a Partially-Polarized EM Plane Wave Traveling in the +z Direction  

This module presents the position of the electric vector of partially-polarized electromagnetic wave during the one period of oscillation when it is travelling in the +z direction (out of the screen, to the observer). Such a wave can be presented as a vector summation of fully polarized wave with controllable parameters and completely non-polarized wave. The degree of polarization defined as a ratio between total power of fully-polarized component to the total power of the wave. If it equals to 0, the wave is completely non-polarized, as it equals to 1 - the wave is fully polarized with parameters of the polarization ellips (orientation and ellipticity angles) that are also shown above the plot.
