Code 2.5 OK Reflection by Two-Layer Composite: Angular Dependence 

This module computes the reflection properties of a two-layer composite with planar boundaries. Medium 1 is air/vacuum with ϵ1 =1.
The thickness of the medium 1, and the frequency in GHz also are inputs.
The reflection coefficient and reflectivity are plotted against the incident angle on the top layer, for both H and V polarizations.
