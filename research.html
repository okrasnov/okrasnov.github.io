Research

Projects

Currently running

  • Radar polarimetry using non-symmetrical MIMO arrays
  • EFRO - Airside 4D weather and air motion mapping using fast-scanning phase-array Doppler FMCW radar. Funded as a part of the Airport Technology Lab research activities.
  • The analysis of polarimetric micro-Doppler features for drones classification .
  • Algorithms for multichannel polarimetric detection, tracking and classification of multiple moving cars on highway using real PARSAX Doppler data.

Past

  • Radar Signal Processing on Flexible/Reconfigurable Computer Architectures. Funded by the King Abdulaziz city for Science and Technology (KACST) in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. 2015-2020.
  • Acquisition and transfer of Drones/PARSAX recordings. - Thales-France (TR6) funded project. 2018-2019.
  • S4DRIVE:Standardized Self-diagnostic Sensing Systems for Highly Automated Driving. NWO/TTW funded project. 2015-2019.
  • Analysis and modeling of radar signals backscattered by wind turbines. CIFRE (France) funded project. 2017-2019.
  • WAMTI: Wideband Algorithms for Migrating Targets Indication: Study using the PARSAX Radar. STW funded project. 2012-2018.
  • Acquisition and transfer of Windfarm/PARSAX recordings. - Thales-France (TR6) funded project. 2014.
  • UFO: UltraFast wind sensOrs for wake-vortex hazards mitigation. - EU FP7 project. 2012-2016.
  • SHINE: Sensing Heterogeneous Information Network Environment. - DIRECT funded project. 2012-2016.
  • RAEBELL: Distributed radar for low-level airspace surveillance. - 2012-2016.
  • STARS: Sensor Technology Applied in Reconfigurable systems sustainable Security.- FES project, 2011-2016.
  • PARSAX: Polarimetric Agile Radar in S- And X-band. - STW funded, 2006-2011
  • Synergy of anchor stations and satellites for the validation of space-borne cloud radars: application to water clouds - SRON funded, GO-2005/014, 2008-2011
  • CLOUDNET: A network of stations for the continuous evaluation of cloud and aerosol profiles in operational NWP models. EU FP5 project. 2001-2005.
  • Radar-lidar synergy for space-based retrievals of water cloud parameters. - SRON funded, EO-060, 2003-2006.
  • Retrieval algorithms of micro-physical cloud parameters for the Earth Radiation Mission. - SRON funded, EO-035, 1999-2003.
