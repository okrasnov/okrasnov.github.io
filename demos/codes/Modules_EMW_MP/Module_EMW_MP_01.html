Code EMW MP-01. Propagation above flat Earth surface Relative Received Power as a function of the receiver antenna height 

 This module computes the received power in received antenna position as a function of the received antenna height. The reflection coefficient from the surface assumed to be equal to -1.
The power normalized in dB scale relatively to the free space porpagation case.
